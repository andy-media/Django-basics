Brain Battle Quiz(BBQ) National STEAM Championship is an exciting practical based applied science, tech, engineering, arts and Maths(STEAM) programme aimed at stirring interest in STEAM among children at the basic school level, with the focus of discovering, inspiring and nurturing future scientists for Africa whiles inculcating problem solving skills such as critical thinking, team work and confidence.

Since its inception in 2018, the BBQ has discovered and nurtured several young talents and has inspired many to pursue excellence. Every year the BBQ directly impacts 1000s of contestants (learners), coaches (facilitators) and school owners and indirectly impacts over 100,000 children, school alumni, educationists and families across the nation and the globe.
